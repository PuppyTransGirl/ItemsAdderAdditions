---
title: Clear Item
description: Remove items from an entity's inventory
---

```yaml
clear_item:
  item: my_item
  amount: 2 # Optional (Default value: 1)
```

For `item`, use a vanilla item from [this list](https://jd.papermc.io/paper/org/bukkit/inventory/ItemType.html#field-summary)
or a custom item with `namespace:value` or just `value`.
