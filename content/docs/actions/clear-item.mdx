---
title: Clear Item
description: Remove custom or vanilla items from a player's inventory as an ItemsAdder action.
version: "1.0.0"
---

```yaml
clear_item:
  item: my_item
  amount: 2 # optional, default: 1
```

## Parameters

### `item`

Required. The item to remove. Use a vanilla material from [this list](https://jd.papermc.io/paper/org/bukkit/inventory/ItemType.html#field-summary), or a custom ItemsAdder item with `namespace:id` or just `id`.

### `amount`

Optional. Defaults to `1`. The number of items to remove.
