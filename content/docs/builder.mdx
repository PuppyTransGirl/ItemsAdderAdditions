Pick an action, behaviour, or recipe, fill in the fields, and copy the generated YAML straight into your item config.

<Builder />

- **Actions**: paste the output inside your item's `events` section. See the [action parameters](/docs/actions/parameters) page for a full example.
- **Behaviours**: paste the output directly under your item definition.
- **Recipes**: paste the output into any ItemsAdder content YML file (the full `info` + `recipes` block is included).
