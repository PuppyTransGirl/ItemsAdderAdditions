**ItemsAdderAdditions** extends [ItemsAdder](https://itemsadder.com/) with extra actions, behaviours, and
features - configured directly inside your existing YML files. It now resolves ItemsAdder content, MMOItems items, vanilla Minecraft items/blocks, and vanilla tags through the same compatibility layer. No restart needed, just `/iareload`.

## Requirements

- **ItemsAdder** (latest recommended)
- **Paper** 1.20.6+
- **MMOItems** *(optional, only required for `mmoitems:type:id` references)*

## Compatibility

See [Compatibility and IDs](/docs/en/compatibility) for the accepted formats for ItemsAdder IDs, MMOItems IDs, vanilla item/block IDs, and vanilla tags.
