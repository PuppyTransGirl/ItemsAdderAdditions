---
title: Clear Item
description: Remove ItemsAdder, MMOItems, or vanilla items from a player inventory.
version: "1.0.0"
---

```yaml
clear_item:
  item: my_item
  amount: 2 # optional, default: 1
```

## Parameters

### `item`

Required. The item to remove. Use a vanilla material, an ItemsAdder item with `namespace:id` or just `id`, or an MMOItems item with `mmoitems:type:id`. Tags are not supported for this parameter because the action needs one concrete item. See [IDs](/en/docs/compatibility/ids).

### `amount`

Optional. Defaults to `1`. The number of items to remove.
