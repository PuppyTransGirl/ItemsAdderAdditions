---
title: Title
description: Show a title and subtitle on screen as an ItemsAdder action.
version: "1.0.0"
---

```yaml
title:
  title: "<bold><gold>Welcome!"
  subtitle: "<gray>Enjoy your stay" # optional
  fade_in: 5 # optional, default: 10 ticks
  stay: 40 # optional, default: 70 ticks
  fade_out: 30 # optional, default: 20 ticks
```

## Parameters

### `title`

Required. The large title text. Supports [MiniMessage](https://docs.papermc.io/adventure/minimessage/format/), [PlaceholderAPI](https://modrinth.com/plugin/placeholderapi), and font images.

### `subtitle`

Optional. The smaller text shown below the title. Supports MiniMessage, PlaceholderAPI, and font images.

### `fade_in`

Optional. Defaults to `10` ticks. Fade-in duration.

### `stay`

Optional. Defaults to `70` ticks. How long the title stays on screen.

### `fade_out`

Optional. Defaults to `20` ticks. Fade-out duration.
