---
title: Swing Hand
description: Trigger a main-hand or off-hand swing animation on a player or entity using an ItemsAdder action. Configurable per-hand in your item YAML.
version: "1.0.0"
---

```yaml
swing_hand:
  hand: "off_hand"
```

## Parameters

### `hand`

Optional. Defaults to `hand`. Which hand to swing: `hand` or `off_hand`.
